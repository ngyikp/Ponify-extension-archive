Help
Here you can modify general settings and restore the defaults.

The highlighting option can be used to highlight text that has been ponified. You can select the colour that is used by entering any valid 3 or 6 digit hexadecimal colour code.

When enabled, the tooltips option will show the original (non-ponified) text when you hover your mouse over any text that has been ponified.

Highlighting and Tooltips will slow ponify down slightly, and it is possible that they may have unintended results on some websites. The change in performance should be unnoticeable on modern computers.

If you do notice a loss of performance, or find that large pages cause the browser to momentarily freeze, try enabling "Pseudo Threading."
Here you can modify the list of words that will be replaced on websites. You can add and remove replacements, as well as edit current replacements. Should you wish to restore the default settings, navigate to the General Options tab and press the "Restore Defaults" under "Replacement List".

To add a new replacement, click the "Add Row" button. A new row will appear in the list where you can enter the new data.

To remove one or more words from the list, select the relevant check boxes in the first column and click the "Delete Checked" button. To select all of the items in the list, check the box in the top row.

To edit any of the entries, click the word that you wish to change, and type your alteration.
Here you can configure which websites are ponified and which ones aren't. There are two methods for configuring this: You can either have a blacklist or a whitelist. A blacklist means that the websites you specify in the list will not be ponified, and all others will. A whitelist means that the websites you specify in the list will be ponified, and all others won't.

To add a website to the list, click the "Add Row" button. A new row will appear where you can enter the new data.

To remove one or more websites from the list, select the relevant check boxes in the first column and click the "Delete Checked" button. To select all of the items in the list, check the box in the top row.

To edit any of the entries, click the entry that you wish to change and type your alteration.
